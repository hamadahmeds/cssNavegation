Gulf – Seattle Partners (GSP)

Gulf – Seattle Partners (GSP) was founded in 2016 after Professor Murphy returning to Seattle from the Gulf after 29 years of higher education, in Oman, the UAE, and Qatar where he was a professor of chemistry. His 49 years of tertiary and secondary has given him experience in international and national educational systems. He has expertise in air quality monitoring and operated an air quality monitoring station in Doha, Qatar while working at Carnegie Mellon University – Qatar.

GSP links science and education professionals in the Gulf with professionals in the greater Seattle area to further scientific and educational ventures, with the hopes of establishing significant research opportunities between the two geographical regions.
